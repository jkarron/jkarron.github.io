Our Favorite Movies

by Julia Karron

There are movies we think are good and movies we all pay to go see. These movies are not always the same. This is a visual look at our favorite movies, using IMDB ratings and data of box office ticket sales (adjusted for inflation) from Box Office Mojo.

Top Grossing Films Per Decade

One way to rate how good a film is to look at how much money it made.

The films in this slideshow were the highest grossing in their decade, starting from the 1920's until the 2010's.

Top 20 International Box Office Market Countries

A second way to quantify how good a movie was is to examine it on an international scale.

This map shows the top 20 international box office countries, including that countrie's population and international box office sales in billions.

How Good Was Each Star Wars Trilogy?

The original Star Wars Trilogy is considered by many to be one of the greatest trilogies of all time.

But how does it fare against the other Star Wars Trilogies?

Using the adjusted gross income for each movie in each series in addition to their IMDB ranking, we can rank each trilogy against each other.
