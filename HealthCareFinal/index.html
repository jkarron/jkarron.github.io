Maryland's Increase in Stage 1 Breast Cancer Rates

by Julia Karron

An increase in stage 1 breast cancer rates means catching deadly cancers earlier and saving more women's lives. This story -- told through graphics -- documents how the Affordable Care Act's preventive service mandate and 3-D mammography helped women.
